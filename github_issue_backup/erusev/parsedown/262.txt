    "body": "I can give you some directions.\r\n\r\nWhat you describe is an inline element. Most inline elements have markers. Markers are the first character of inline elements. In the case of your inline element, the marker is `{`.\r\n\r\nHere's what you could do:\r\n\r\n1. In the constructor of your extension, add an element to the `$this->InlineTypes` array. The key of that element should be the marker of your inline element (`{`) and the value should be the name of your inline element (e.g. `ColoredText`);\r\n\r\n2. Create a method named `inlineColoredText`. This method will get called whenever the parser finds the marker `{` in the text.\r\n\r\n3. Have a look at the other `inline...` methods to figure out how to implement it."
