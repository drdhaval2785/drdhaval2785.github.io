1 abela -> abela NO CHANGE
[Other dictionaries spell as avela, but, by alph. order, gst's 'abela' was intentional.]

2 anekArTasaNgraha -> anekArTasaNgraha NO CHANGE
[use of homorganic nasal. Others spell as anekArTasaMgraha]
anekArTasaNgraha Tatpur. m. (-haH) The title of the second part

of Hemachandra's vocabulary, containing words with their

various meanings. E. anekArTa and saMgraha.
3 antaHsaYjYa -> antaHsaYjYa NO CHANGE
[homorganic nasal]
antaHsaYjYa Bahuvr. m. f. n. (-YjYaH-YjYA-YjYam) Sensitive

(said of plants in general which, according to the native

theory, from being affected by the quality of darkness or

sinfulness — tamas — are merely capable of sensation or

of agreeable and disagreeable feelings, but not of exterior

manifestations), e. g. tamasA bahurUpeRa cezwitAH karmahetunA .

antaHsaMjYA Bavantyete suKaduHKasamanvitAH; comp. antaScEtanya.

E. antar and saMjYA.
4 anvarTasaYjYa -> anvarTasaYjYa NO CHANGE
[homorganic nasal]
5 apaSugGna -> apaSugGna NO CHANGE
[Others dictionaries have paSuGna. Is there a grammar rule allowing optional 'gG' here?]

6 apfTaggrahaRa -> apfTaggrahaRa NO CHANGE
[PW has pfTaggrahaRa]
apfTaggrahaRa Tatpur. n. (-Ram) Not taking or perceiving se-

parately; e. g. in the Nyáya Sútra: tadASrayatvAdapfTaggrahaRam

‘there is no perception separately (of a web from its

threads)’, i. e. a web cannot be perceived separately from

its threads, since it consists thereof. E. a neg. and pfTa-

ggrahaRa.
7 apramUrcCita -> apramUrcCita NO CHANGE
[BHS has pramUrcCita]
apramUrcCita Tatpur. m. f. n. (-taH-tA-tam) The same as the

preceding (of which it occurs as an explanation). E. a

neg. and pramUrcCita.

10 abBrIya -> abBrIya NO CHANGE
[Note: The dictionary ends abruptly, before aBrIya]
abBrIya m. f. n. (-yaH-yA-yam) See aBrIya.
