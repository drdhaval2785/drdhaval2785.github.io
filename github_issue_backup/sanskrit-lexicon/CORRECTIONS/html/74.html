2 anekArTasaNgraha -> anekArTasaNgraha NO CHANGE [use of homorganic nasal. Others spell as anekArTasaMgraha] anekArTasaNgraha Tatpur. m. (-haH) The title of the second part of Hemachandra's vocabulary, containing words with their various meanings. E. anekArTa and saMgraha.

3 antaHsaYjYa -> antaHsaYjYa NO CHANGE [homorganic nasal] antaHsaYjYa Bahuvr. m. f. n. (-YjYaH-YjYA-YjYam) Sensitive (said of plants in general which, according to the native theory, from being affected by the quality of darkness or sinfulness — tamas — are merely capable of sensation or of agreeable and disagreeable feelings, but not of exterior manifestations), e. g. tamasA bahurUpeRa cezwitAH karmahetunA . antaHsaMjYA Bavantyete suKaduHKasamanvitAH; comp. antaScEtanya. E. antar and saMjYA.

5 apaSugGna -> apaSugGna NO CHANGE [Others dictionaries have paSuGna. Is there a grammar rule allowing optional 'gG' here?]

6 apfTaggrahaRa -> apfTaggrahaRa NO CHANGE [PW has pfTaggrahaRa] apfTaggrahaRa Tatpur. n. (-Ram) Not taking or perceiving se- parately; e. g. in the Nyáya Sútra: tadASrayatvAdapfTaggrahaRam ‘there is no perception separately (of a web from its threads)’, i. e. a web cannot be perceived separately from its threads, since it consists thereof.
