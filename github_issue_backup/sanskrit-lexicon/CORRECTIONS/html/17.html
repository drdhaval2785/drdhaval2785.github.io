funderburkjim commented on 2014-10-28 23:20:32

The entries should remain as they are. Usually, MW has the 'sandhi' form of the headword as separate entries.

These are informally classified by me as <see> entries - meaning that they are 'extra' entries that, for one reason or another, MW added to the dictionary; however, they only point to other entries, the 'body' of the entries only contain information 'see X'.

In the spirit of Marcis's Linnaean classification of all headwords in MW, it would probably be an improvement to develop some incremental markup that would readily identify these and other 'pseudo-entries'.
