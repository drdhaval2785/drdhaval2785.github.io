GRA corrections drawn from alphabetical misorderings

funderburkjim commented on 2015-01-29 01:28:46

It is believed that most of the 235 alphabetical misorderings of headwords in the Grassman Dictionary are due to typographical errors (sometimes due to print inaccuracies) or to erroneous markup of the many prefix subparts of headwords.

This issue is devoted to examination of these cases. The comments are generated by program, and contain links to the Cologne displays and scans for each case. [Note: For cases where the headword is wrong, and after the correction is made, then the link to the Cologne display will likely fail (since it is based on the old, incorrect spelling)].
