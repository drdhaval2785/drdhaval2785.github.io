funderburkjim commented on 2014-11-22 00:55:50

Re-examine SAliBaYjikAy => SAliBaYjikAya . The difference is that final 'a'. MW does have this (as SAla...Aya). Both PW and MW are denominative verb forms. I think that final 'a' is just a difference in convention between PW and MW regarding the citation of a denominative verb. Conclusion : Leave PW as it is in this case.
