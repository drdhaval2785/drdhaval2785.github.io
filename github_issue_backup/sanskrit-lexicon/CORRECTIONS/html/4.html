Convention for adding 'tva' to words ending with 'n'

drdhaval2785 commented on 2014-10-07 17:02:57

Entries like bahvAsintva leKahArintva vizayintva and vyaBicArintva in MW have prompted me to draw attention to one erroneous data entry convention in MW.
For words ending with 'n' like 'vyaBicArin' - the suffix of 'tva' would give the word as 'vyaBicAritva' (with elision of 'n'.)

Please check for such occurrences in all dictionaries where particularly 'intva' pattern is found.
